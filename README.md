StudyBuddy is a collaborative study platform for UNC students to create course-specific study groups, share resources, and utilize AI-powered study tools.

## Features

**Group Creation and Join System** - Create study groups for specific courses and invite classmates via shareable links or searchable directory.

**Shared Notes Board** - Post text notes and upload files (PDFs, images) to a centralized board that all group members can access in real-time.

**Real-time Group Chat** - Chat with group members and see who's online with live presence indicators.

**AI Study Helper** - Generate summaries, quiz questions, and flashcards from your notes using AI (supports Google Gemini and OpenAI).

**Personal Dashboard** - View your groups, track recent activity, manage study streaks, and quickly access your study materials.
